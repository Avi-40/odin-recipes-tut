Classic Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a hearty and comforting Italian dish. Layers of perfectly cooked lasagna noodles, savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella come together to create a delicious and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook 12 lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, brown 1 pound of ground beef (or Italian sausage), add onion and garlic, and sauté until onion is translucent.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, sugar, basil, salt, oregano, pepper, and parsley. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine 15 ounces of ricotta cheese, 1 egg, and parsley.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Spread a thin layer of sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  7. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese. Repeat.
  8. Finish with a final layer of noodles, sauce, and cheeses.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes.
  10. Let it rest for 10-15 minutes before serving.
  11. Garnish with fresh parsley, if desired, and serve hot.
